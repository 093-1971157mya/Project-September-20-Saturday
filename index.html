Why Lisbon?

Lisbon, the city of seven hills

The legend has it that Lisbon was named Olissipo by the Romans for its resemblance to the Roman capital. Just like Rome, Lisbon began as a humble settlement nestled among seven hills.

Beyond the modern shops and peaceful cafes, you can wander the seven hills and see the city from above — with terracotta rooftops, white walls, and the blue sea on the horizon.
